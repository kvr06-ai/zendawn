My Story

Where science meets wanderlust

Hello! I'm Kaushik Rajan

Scientist, researcher, and traveler

I'm a scientist and researcher with nearly a decade in tech, including four years at Amazon. My research focuses on reinforcement learning, game theory and decision science. Visit my technical blog for insights and tools in these areas.

Beyond algorithms, I'm passionate about exploring new cultures and learning languages. My travels through various countries have complemented my scientific mindset with a deeper appreciation for cultural diversity.

This travel blog documents my experiences around the world. I aim to provide practical insights for fellow travelers while sharing authentic encounters with local traditions and communities.

From spiritual retreats in India to urban explorations in the US, I hope my experiences help you discover these destinations for yourself.
